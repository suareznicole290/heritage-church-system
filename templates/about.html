About the Heritage Churches of Bohol System

{% if is_admin_view %} This platform supports heritage documentation, disaster monitoring, and risk-informed administrative decision-making for significant churches across Bohol. {% else %} This platform was developed to support heritage documentation, disaster monitoring, and risk-informed decision-making for significant churches across Bohol. It serves as a centralized information system for preserving cultural heritage while improving disaster preparedness and public awareness. {% endif %}

Project Purpose

The Heritage Churches of Bohol — DRRM Information System was designed to help organize and manage important data related to heritage churches in the province. It combines cultural documentation with disaster risk reduction and management functions in one accessible platform.

Through this system, users can view church profiles, monitor hazard-related information, browse public disaster reports, and access mapped church locations. Administrators can also maintain records such as descriptions, heritage recognitions, hazard assessments, image galleries, and incident reports.

The system aims to strengthen preservation efforts by making heritage information easier to document, update, analyze, and share with both authorized users and the public.

Core Features
Church Records
Maintain church profiles, location details, and monitoring status.
Hazard Mapping
Visualize churches on an interactive map with risk-based filtering.
Disaster Reports
Track incidents, descriptions, statuses, and supporting photos.
Heritage Documentation
Store descriptions, significance, recognitions, and image galleries.
Administrative Monitoring
Support role-based access, audit logging, and record management.
